Photoshop Crash Course

In this course, I will teach you the Photoshop basics in a short amount of time; in approximately two hours I will teach you:

  • Photoshop Basics and UI
  • Using the Sidebar Tools
  • Working with Layers and Masks
  • Various Photoshop Techniques
  • Quick Photo Editing
  • Exporting and Preparing Images for various purposes

This course is intended as a gateway to self-learning, giving you a head start in the field of photo manipulation. After the course you will know how to properly edit photos in a manner which is not destructive to the original image. This course is one-to-one, where the student is given individual attention.

I am a student which has been using Photoshop and other Adobe Suite Programs for years, you can view some of my work below, and on the rest of this Portfolio Website. I will teach you for as long as it takes until you fully understand, even if it takes a bit over two hours, without changing the rate.

Group Offer: The course can also be done as a group, for each person present (up to a group of 5), €1 will be deducted from each participant's bill.

Languages spoken: English, Italian, Maltese.
Qualifications: Diploma in Design Foundation Studies at the University of Malta.

Note: No Qualification Certificate will be provided upon completion of this course. This course is a private one-on-one lecture.
